# Core Modules

## Customer & Party Management

- Unified customer profile (individual, corporate, related parties)
- KYC/AML screening workflows
- Risk profiling and periodic review cadence
- Document management and retention policies

## Product Factory

- Product templates (deposit, loan, treasury instruments)
- Configurable pricing rules, fees, and eligibility constraints
- Parameter-driven interest accrual and posting schedules
- Versioned product definitions with audit history

## Account Management

- Account lifecycle (open, suspend, close)
- Multi-currency support and balance management
- Ledger posting rules and account controls

## Lending & Credit

- Loan origination and approval workflow
- Disbursement scheduling and repayment plans
- Collateral tracking and valuation
- Collections and delinquency processing

## Payments & Transfers

- Internal transfers and book entries
- External payment rails integration
- Cutoff rules, settlement, and reversals
- Standing instructions and batch processing

## General Ledger & Finance

- Chart of accounts and mapping rules
- Auto-posting from sub-ledgers
- Financial close and reconciliation

## Compliance & Risk

- AML monitoring and alerting
- Limits and exposure monitoring
- Regulatory reporting workflows

## Operations & Administration

- Maker-checker workflows
- Audit logging and evidence retention
- Configurable entitlements and roles
