# BSP Compliance Checkpoints

> This document is a planning artifact and should be refined with specific BSP circulars and reporting requirements.

## Governance & Risk Management

- Board-approved risk policies and documented controls
- Segregation of duties (maker-checker)
- Regular risk assessments and control testing

## KYC / AML

- Customer due diligence workflows
- Sanctions and watchlist screening
- Transaction monitoring and alert escalation
- Suspicious transaction reporting processes

## Data Privacy & Security

- Data classification and retention policies
- Encryption key management and HSM usage
- Access control reviews and privileged access monitoring

## Transaction Integrity

- Immutable audit logs for all financial events
- Dual control for sensitive operations
- Reconciliation routines and exception handling

## Regulatory Reporting

- Scheduled BSP report generation
- Data lineage and reconciliation for report sources
- Evidence packs for audits and examinations
